Ingredients
- 10 green or oolong tea bags
- 2-inch piece fresh ginger peeled and cut into thin slices
- 3 large sprigs mint
- 1 small lemon, sliced
- 4 cups boiling water


Directions:
1. Combine tea bags, fresh ginger, mint, and lemon slices in heatproof 2-qt pitcher.
2. Pour in the boiling water. Stir once and let tea bags steep 6 minutes. Remove and discard tea bags and mint sprigs.
3. Cool for 20 minutes.
4. Add enough ice and cold water to make 6 cups. Serve over ice in glasses with fresh mint sprigs and lemon slices.
